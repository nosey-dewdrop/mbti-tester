# mbti-tester
local storage, github pages deploy, i'll code it because i am really bored of collecting all the assets for my other projects. 

a field guide to figuring out someone's mbti without making them take the test. because let's be real, you can't just hand someone a 16 personalities link on the third date. some people do it anyways. it was lovely to know you more. thank you... 

this project is purely built on personal experience and painful observations. like after many bad meeting, dates etc. i used to wonder why this person acted this way? actually i should have seen this coming. i should have observed them more so that i would know what their next act is. mbti, or any thing that portrays someones charater in a spectrum is good to know i think. of course 10b people living only at this moment are not similar but it is a spectrum. like, i am lilac and you are violet. 

## what is this?

you meet someone. you like them. or maybe you're just trying to convey a group project with them. either way what's their mbti? you don't know. you can't make them take the test. so you observe. does this person sacrifice everything for their goals? or they are so absurd, dramatical that you consider them being really mentally retarded or really genius? (me,kurt cobain, tolkien, arwen, syd barret, jim morrison, arthur fleck. we both are infp) do they flinch when you say something unexpected, or do they laugh and adapt? when plans change, do they roll with it or say "you think too fast"? these are the questions. 
this site helps you read the answers.

## pages

- **home** — the manifesto. why this exists.
- **what is mbti?** — but my version, not wikipedia's.
- **type pages** — observation cards, red/green flags, damla's notes, pixel art avatars for each type.

## tech

- vanilla html / css / js
- pixel art assets (generated with pixellab)
- localstorage for saving your observations
- deployed on github pages

## assets

all assets are pixel art, consistent grid (32x32 base), `image-rendering: pixelated` everywhere.
- horizontal pixel dividers / separators
- section frame borders (rpg menu box style corners)
- navigation buttons
- 16 mbti type avatars / icons
